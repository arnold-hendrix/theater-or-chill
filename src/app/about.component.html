About

TheaterOrChill offers ticketing and rental services for movies showing in theaters. For the same price, you can watch the latest movies at a theater near you, or at the comfort of your home.

Every day it seems more likely that the near future will require us to learn how to live with the COVID-19 pandemic. We at TheaterOrChill understand that movie enthusiasts around the world want to be able to watch new movies as they are released, even as inconveniences caused by COVID-19 lockdown measures and safety protocols continue to impact their lives.

That is why we have launched a platform where movie fans can decide to rent or go see a movie in theaters at no extra cost. Movie rentals are valid while the movies are in theaters. In other words, once you have rented a movie, you have it available to you for as long as it is available on our website! You can stream it as many times as you want by yourself, or with your family and friends. Isn't that cool?

Having said that, we are not discouraging going to the movies! And that's why those of you who decide to watch a movie in theaters will earn rewards on every visit. After four visits, you will have enough rewards to get yourself a free ticket to your fifth visit, and a $15 voucher that can be applied to food, drinks, and movie memorabilia at a theater near you.

Anyway, thanks for stopping by and hopefully we will see you again.
